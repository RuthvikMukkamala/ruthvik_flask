GLASS Research Initiative

MISSION STATEMENT

Building neural networks to solve partial differential equations to improve financial markets, with a focus on mathematical rigor and practical applications.

RESEARCH OVERVIEW

The GLASS project investigates the intersection of deep learning and quantitative finance, specifically focusing on:

• Neural network architectures optimized for solving high-dimensional PDEs in financial modeling

• Novel optimization techniques for improved convergence in complex market scenarios

• Implementation of robust numerical methods for real-time market analysis

Neural Architecture

• Multi-layer perceptrons with adaptive activation functions

• Residual connections for improved gradient flow

• Custom loss functions incorporating financial domain knowledge

Optimization Framework

• Adaptive learning rate schemes

• Curriculum learning for complex PDE systems

• Batch normalization techniques for stable training
