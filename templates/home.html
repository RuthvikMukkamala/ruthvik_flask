Hi, my name is Ruthvik Mukkamala.

I like quantitative research for financial markets, machine learning, and computer vision.

In university I started Roebling Research LLC, a small fund to test quantitative equity strategies and fundamental investing. I recently shut down to focus on AI/LLM development. My 2023 performance was 30%, 2024 performance was 25%. Trade receipts available upon request. I ran a diversified, sector agnostic strategy that had low correlation between positions. I strongly believe in factor investing, and am comfortable with using macroeconomic indicators to inform investment decisions.

The ability to use AI/LLM tools will be an existential component for the future of investing. I interpret finance as a quasi-science - a good analogy we have of our current understanding of finance is that of a periodic table of elements. We can understand certain properties and relationship between assets, but we are limited to conclusive understanding by the data we have. AI/LLM tools are the way to unlock this new frontier of finance through the ability to analyze more structured/unstructured data sets. I believe that we will get to a significantly better approximation of factor investing through the use of AI/LLM tools.

After graduating from university, I will be joining a NX1 Capital L.P founded by very talented fundamental and discretionary investors where I will help build LLM tools.

I have also shared my trained Physics Informed Neural Networks paper on equity options to improve research for options pricing.

I have also previously worked as a developer for the Macro Quant Development Team at Balyasny Asset Management in 2023. I also worked at HIFI, a music-tech startup acquired by Block in 2022 as a software engineering intern. I loved the focus of a lean team passionate about building financial services for a targeted audience.
